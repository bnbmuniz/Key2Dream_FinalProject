  • About the Project

    This is the final project of Ironhack Data Analytics Bootcamp. The motivation of the project comes from personal interest in art and in what dreams can represent.

    The name of the website is inspired on the famous painting of René Magritte "Key to Dreams", that became a big step for his achievements in the Surrealist movement. With the help of Deep Learning, Key2Dream Project is about creating from realistic to surreal drawings that come from our creative minds when dreaming.
